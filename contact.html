Mission Statement

Our mission statement is to increase awareness of Domestic Violence and provide resources to support victims of abuse, so they can lead a better life.

These are some resources that can help you if you are experiencing Domestic Violence.

Don't listen to anyone who tells you the abuse is your fault. You can't control your partner. But you can take steps to be safer.
